WHO ARE WE

We are a group of industry professionals who believe that with the right mix of training, guidance and preparation, every student can secure the job of his or dreams. The market is always brimming with opportunities for those who are willing to upskill themselves, and you are no exception to that rule. Even if you have missed out on an opportunity then you need not fret, for you can always prepare for the next one… And at Techlist, we help you in doing just that, preparing for the right opportunities.

With a team that has worked with the placement cells of IITs, IIMs and numerous other reputed institutions, and an expert pool comprising of industry veterans with decades of experience in training and guidance, Techlist will equip you with just the right tools for acing your recruitment process. We work closely with both students as well as institutions to ensure that our students are able to avail the best of opportunities available to them.Be it consulting with students on picking up the right career, or helping them build their professional profile or conducting mock placement drives, we provide a range of services to support students in their quest for their dream jobs.
